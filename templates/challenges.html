Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I fail the challenge?

If you fail the challenge by breaching loss limits or not reaching the profit target, you can purchase the challenge again. Many successful traders take multiple attempts.

When do I get paid?

Once you become a funded trader, you'll receive profit share payments every 2 weeks (bi-weekly). Your first payout will be 14 days after your first profitable trade.

Can I trade on weekends?

Yes! Weekend trading is allowed on all our challenges. You can trade 24/5 (Monday 00:00 to Friday 23:59 server time).

What trading strategies are allowed?

All trading strategies are allowed including scalping, hedging, and news trading. The only restrictions are the loss limits and minimum trading days.
