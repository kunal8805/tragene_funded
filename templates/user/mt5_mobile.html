❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q I cannot login

Verify your login ID, password and exact server name. Make sure you've selected the correct server from the list.

Q MT5 says no connection

Check your internet connection and restart MT5. If the issue persists, try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data.

Q Server not found

Enter the exact server name received in your email. Use the search bar and type the name precisely as shown.

Q Charts not loading

Open Market Watch, tap the menu, and choose Show All. This will display all available trading symbols.

Q Where are my credentials?

Credentials are automatically sent to your verified email after challenge purchase. Check your inbox, spam, and promotions folders.
