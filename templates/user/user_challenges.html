Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I reach the profit target?

Once you reach 12% profit target with minimum 5 trading days, you automatically qualify for the funded account.

What if I exceed the daily loss limit?

If you lose 5% of your account in a single day, the challenge ends immediately and you'll need to purchase a new one.

How long do I have to complete the challenge?

You have 30 calendar days to reach the profit target. If time expires, the challenge ends.

When do I get paid?

After passing the challenge, you'll receive your profit share biweekly from your trading profits.

Can I retry if I fail?

Yes! You can purchase the same or different challenge again if you don't pass on your first attempt.

Need more help?

Contact our support team at support@tragene.com or use the live chat on the dashboard.
