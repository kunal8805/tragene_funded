📱 How to Reduce Your Image Size

On Your Phone

  1. 1. Open your Camera or Gallery app
  2. 2. Tap Edit on the photo
  3. 3. Look for Resize or Compress option
  4. 4. Select Medium or Small size
  5. 5. Save and re-upload

Take a Screenshot Instead

Screenshots are usually much smaller than camera photos.

iPhone

Press Side + Volume Up

Android

Press Power + Volume Down

Crop Unnecessary Parts

Remove extra background. Only keep the document visible. This reduces file size significantly.

Quick Tips

  • ✅ Use JPEG/JPG format instead of PNG (smaller size)
  • ✅ Keep resolution around 1920x1080 max
  • ✅ Make sure the document is clearly readable
  • ✅ Screenshots of the document photo also work well
